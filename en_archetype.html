Entrepreneurial Archetypes

Understand your natural tendencies and how to work with them.

Overthinker

Thinking replaces doing. Waiting for certainty that never arrives.

Starter

Starting feels easy. Finishing becomes harder once the novelty fades.

Busy Worker

Lots of activity, but not always the right leverage.

Hesitator

Protecting safety can quietly block momentum and commitment.

Switcher

Changing direction too often prevents results from compounding.

Balanced Founder

Steady progress, but sometimes limited by playing too safely.
