Frequently asked questions

How is this different from coaching?

Coaching is open‑ended and exploratory. En‑boarding is a structured, milestone‑driven process with concrete deliverables. You get a roadmap, not just motivation. Think of it as an onboarding program for your startup.

What if I'm not ready to pay?

Join the free weekly meetup first – it's a low‑pressure way to see if it's for you. Or apply for the free pilot.

How long does it take?

Each session is 90 minutes. The full process can be completed in 2‑4 weeks, but you go at your own pace.

Do I need a team?

No, this is designed for solo founders and early‑stage entrepreneurs.
