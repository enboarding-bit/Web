Clarity

Your pain point: struggling to define or validate the right direction

Overthinker

Pattern: Thinking replaces doing.
Failure: Waiting for certainty that never arrives.
Consequence: Missed opportunities, delayed launch.
Untapped Advantage: You see patterns others miss.
Superpower: Strong analytical thinking.
Key Tool: Decision constraint (limit options to force action).

Lost Executor

Pattern: Waiting for the right path.
Failure: Unused capacity because direction is unclear.
Consequence: Energy scattered, no momentum.
Untapped Advantage: You execute with precision when direction is clear.
Superpower: Reliable execution.
Key Tool: Clear‑path project charter (one‑page directive).

Starter

Pattern: Starting without finishing.
Failure: Loss of interest after novelty fades.
Consequence: Many beginnings, no outcomes.
Untapped Advantage: You generate fresh momentum effortlessly.
Superpower: Creative ignition.
Key Tool: Idea‑to‑launch sprint (time‑boxed finish line).

Direction Stability

Your pain point: struggling to stay on one direction long enough for results to compound

Hesitator

Pattern: Protecting safety over growth.
Failure: Fear of loss prevents commitment.
Consequence: Preparation without action.
Untapped Advantage: You protect resources and avoid major mistakes.
Superpower: Risk intelligence.
Key Tool: Controlled experiment framework (small reversible bets).

Switcher

Pattern: Constant switching.
Failure: Never staying long enough for results to compound.
Consequence: Shallow progress across many directions.
Untapped Advantage: You test many possibilities quickly.
Superpower: Versatility.
Key Tool: Compound experiment tracker (capture learnings, double down).

Wanderer

Pattern: Avoiding commitment to keep options open.
Failure: Flexibility without direction.
Consequence: Nothing fully moves forward.
Untapped Advantage: You stay open to the best opportunities.
Superpower: Freedom of perspective.
Key Tool: Temporary constraint canvas (30‑day focus container).

Leverage

Your pain point: struggling to turn effort into meaningful results

Idealist

Pattern: Meaning without execution.
Failure: Hesitation when things feel commercial.
Consequence: Purpose without sustainability.
Untapped Advantage: You build things that genuinely matter.
Superpower: Deep purpose.
Key Tool: Value‑aligned business model canvas (commercialize without compromise).

Busy Worker

Pattern: Activity without progress.
Failure: Effort misaligned with outcomes.
Consequence: Exhaustion without growth.
Untapped Advantage: You out‑effort almost everyone.
Superpower: Relentless work ethic.
Key Tool: Strategic effort matrix (prioritize high‑leverage activities).

Balanced Founder

Pattern: Steady but incremental progress.
Failure: Plateaus from lack of extreme leverage.
Consequence: Scaling blocked.
Untapped Advantage: You combine action, adaptation, and consistency.
Superpower: Sustainable execution.
Key Tool: Scaling leverage audit (identify the one constraint).

What happens next depends on your pattern

If you’re an Overthinker:
You need to break out of analysis and start executing.

If you’re inconsistent:
You need to stabilize your direction.

If you’re already moving:
You may need to pivot toward higher‑impact actions.

Different patterns require different systems.
There is no generic solution.
