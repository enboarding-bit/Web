Idea vs. Business: The 5 Missing Links

TL;DR: An idea is a destination. A business is the vehicle that gets there.

A good idea is not yet a business. There are structural links that have to exist in between:

  • The specific who: not everyone, but a real person with a real pain.
  • The value gap: what problem are you solving in concrete terms?
  • The distribution hook: where do these people already pay attention?
  • The unit economics: what does it cost to reach one customer compared with what they pay?
  • The feedback loop: how do you know early when something is not working?
If more than two links are missing,
you do not have a business yet.
You have an unfinished idea.

Structure is what turns inspiration into something that can actually move.
