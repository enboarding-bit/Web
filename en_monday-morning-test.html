The "Monday Morning" Test

TL;DR: If you have to decide what to work on when you sit down, you have already lost part of the day.

The most productive founders do not have more willpower. They simply do not waste their best energy on deciding what to do after they begin.

The fix is to separate strategy from execution:

  • Sunday: review the roadmap and choose three needle-movers.
  • Monday: execute without reopening the question.

Clarity is not only about vision. It is also about protecting your energy from avoidable decisions.
