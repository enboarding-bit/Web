About En-boarding

Onboarding for entrepreneurs

Because people starting a business deserve the same head start as employees.

Our mission

Most people start with an idea and a lot of energy — but no structure. They piece together advice from blogs, podcasts, and mentors, then wonder why they still feel stuck or overwhelmed.

En-boarding exists to fix that. The goal is to give people a clearer process — the same kind of structured onboarding that employees get at a company, but adapted for the uncertainty of building something from scratch.

What we believe

You do not need more motivation. You need a system. Most people do not struggle because they lack effort. They struggle because the path is unclear and their energy gets scattered.

That is why these sessions focus on clarity, direction, and a practical next step instead of generic inspiration.

Bouchra Driouach

Founder & Strategy Consultant

I work with people who are starting a business and need clarity, strategic direction, and practical execution. These sessions are focused, direct, and designed to help you move forward with better priorities and a clearer plan.
