The "Monday Morning" Test

TL;DR: If you still need to decide what matters when you sit down on Monday morning, the week is already harder than it needs to be.

The most productive founders are not magical. They simply protect their best mental energy from being wasted on basic prioritisation.

If Monday starts with deciding what to do, comparing tasks, looking for notes, or rethinking the plan, execution is already leaking.

Sunday: choose the important work
Monday: execute the chosen work

Strategy first
Execution second

A simple weekly reset is often enough:

  • Review the roadmap.
  • Choose the few tasks that actually move things.
  • Put them into the week before the week begins.

That way, Monday becomes execution time instead of debate time.
